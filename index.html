Classification

I enjoy using classification to sort through all types of text, image and other types of data. Knowing more about your patient or customer groups helps you to better understand their requirements ahead of time. From there, we can give them the best support they need.

Prediction

Who doesn't want to know the future? At least the second best thing is predicting the future based off previous data. From logistic regression to an ensemble graph neural network - neural backed decision forest system, using prediction can not only predict business trends, but save lives!

IOT

I always dreamed of building robots, and now that dream has come true! From robotics to IOT, I enjoy it all. As a hobby, I am working on building a prosthetic hand and a few other projects, which you might be able to buy from my online store soon.
